Data Deletion Policy

This policy outlines how you can request the deletion of your data from our application.

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Log in to your account: Visit our application and log in using your credentials.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings: Go to the "Account Settings" section in your profile.
  3. Request Data Deletion: In the "Account Settings" section, click on the "Delete My Data" or "Delete Account" button.
  4. Confirm Deletion: Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your action.
  5. Deletion Confirmation: Once confirmed, your data will be permanently deleted from our database.

Contact for Assistance

If you experience any issues or cannot locate the "Delete My Data" option, please contact us at support@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Please include the following details to help us identify your account:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username or ID (if applicable)

We will process your request and delete your data within 7 business days.

Important Notes

Data deletion is a permanent action and cannot be reversed. Once your data is deleted, it will not be recoverable.
